About Train-miner

Train-miner is an exciting strategy game that puts you in charge of your own railroad empire. Your mission is to collect valuable resources, transport them efficiently, and expand your network across the map.

With intuitive controls and challenging gameplay, Train-miner offers hours of entertainment for players of all ages. The game combines resource management with strategic planning, creating a unique and addictive experience.

Whether you're a casual gamer looking for a quick session or a strategy enthusiast seeking a deeper challenge, Train-miner offers the perfect balance of accessibility and depth.

How to Play

  • 1. Start by building your first train station and connecting tracks
  • 2. Collect resources from mines and transport them to processing centers
  • 3. Upgrade your trains to increase speed and carrying capacity
  • 4. Expand your railroad network to reach new resource locations
  • 5. Complete challenges to earn bonuses and special trains
